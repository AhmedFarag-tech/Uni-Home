About the University

Helwan International Technological University was established to provide high-quality, technology-focused education in Egypt. Originally known as the Egyptian University of Technology, it was restructured and integrated with Helwan University to offer specialized programs in fields like engineering and applied sciences. The university is dedicated to preparing students for the challenges of the modern workforce by emphasizing practical skills, innovation, and global industry standards.

Intermediate

Our intermediate programs provide students with foundational knowledge in various technical fields, preparing them for higher education in specialized areas like Mechatronics, Cybersecurity, and Data Science.

Degree

We offer undergraduate degrees in various technological disciplines, including Mechatronics, Auto-Tronics, and Information Technology areas like Cybersecurity, Data Science, and Artificial Intelligence. Our curriculum integrates rigorous academics with hands-on experience, preparing students for successful careers.

Post Graduation

Our postgraduate programs provide opportunities to specialize further in fields such as AI, Mechatronics, and Industrial Automation. They focus on research, innovation, and leadership development, aiming to contribute significantly to technological advancements
